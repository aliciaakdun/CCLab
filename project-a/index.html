The Crawlers

Scientific Name:

Crawlers

Discovered by / at:

Alicia Akdun, 2024

Family:

The Crawlers are a group that values each other's well being the most. However, they are an invasive species desctructive to the environment and need to be erradicated on sight.

Habitat:

A sandy underground structure dug by the group of crawlers.

Appearance:

Living crawlers look like blue arrows and travel at rapid speeds. When they die, their bodies turn red. Careful, when large crawlers explode, they release two smaller crawlers into the world.

Key Characteristics:

  • Dig underground paths
  • Panic after explosions
  • Destructive to the environment
  • Invasive species
  • Will not die without human intervention

What we know so far about The Crawlers? Crawlers can be found underground everywhere in the world. They are currently posing a threat to human infrastructures, as their digging impacts dirt and sand density. There have been numerous reports of crawlers swarming after one of their groupmates is destroyed, as well as more crawlers emerging from dead ones. Crawlers do not need food, water, or oxygen to survive, and can withstand an incredible amount of weight and pressure, making them almost impossible to kill. The only known method of destruction is using a small explosive device to blow them up one by one.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Click on the crawlers to make them explode. Other crawlers will swarm towards dead crawlers in a panic, making them predictable targets. Careful, large crawlers will release two smaller crawlers. If you click on large dead crawlers, they will keep spawning smaller crawlers, so do not spam. The goal is to eradicate all crawlers as fast as possible.
